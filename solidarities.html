01

The Color Line & The Mestiza

Talk about how the color lne (WEB Du Bois) is not just a barrier for Black Americans but how it is a conceptual borderland that defines Latinx identity as well. By looking at the struggle for racial legibility, we find a shared solidarity in the rejection of "purity" in favor of lived complexity.

02

Viet Thanh Nguyen: The Refugee & The Displaced

There is a profound solidarity between the Vietnamese refugee experience and the Latinx experience of being "foreigners in our own land." Both cultures deal with a haunting sense of a homeland that is historically erased or physically out of reach.
